about me

I graduated from the University of Washington, working with Ira Kemelmacher-Shilzerman as part of the newly-founded UW Reality Lab studying high-fidelity, life-like video generation techniques. I also spent time at Xevo (ex-Surround.io) working on AI software for cars, and at WellSaid Labs as part of Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence (AI2), working on high-fidelity, life-like voice generation.

It has been an incredible adventure, and Seattle will always have a special place in my heart, as there are many people to thank that have guided me on my wonderful journey.
